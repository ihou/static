TabMarker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

TabMarker is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

Information Collection

TabMarker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely on your local device.

Data Storage

The only data we store is:

  • Your tab marking preferences
  • Button position settings
  • Tab states

All this information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions

TabMarker requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: To manage and interact with browser tabs
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • activeTab: To interact with the current tab

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your browser's built-in security measures.

Third-Party Services

TabMarker does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [Your Contact Information].

Compliance

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store's privacy requirements and data handling guidelines.
