Privacy Policy

Welcome to QuickLivePhoto. We take your privacy and personal information protection very seriously.

Information Collection and Use

We only access your photo library when necessary for:

  • Selecting videos for conversion
  • Saving generated Live Photos

Data Storage

All video processing is performed locally on your device. We do not upload or store any of your media files.

Permissions

The application requires the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: Required for selecting videos and saving Live Photos

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your privacy and personal information. All operations are performed locally without any network transmission.
