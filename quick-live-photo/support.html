Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to select a video?

Click the "Select Video" button to choose a video from your photo library.

2. What video formats are supported?

We support video formats captured by iPhone (MOV, MP4).

3. Why do you need photo library permission?

We need photo library permission to select videos and save generated Live Photos.

4. Where can I find the converted files?

Converted Live Photos will be saved to your photo library and can be viewed in the Photos app.
