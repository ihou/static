Frequently Asked Questions

How do I interact with the cats?

Simply hover your mouse over the cat holes on your desktop. The cats will peek out and play with you!

Can I customize the cat's behavior?

Currently, the cats have their own playful personalities. We're working on adding customization options in future updates.

Does Clowder affect my system performance?

No, Clowder is designed to be lightweight and efficient, using minimal system resources.
