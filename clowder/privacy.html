Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025/02/17

Introduction

Welcome to Clowder. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information collected through our desktop application.

Information We Don't Collect

Clowder is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Any other personal data

App Functionality

Clowder operates entirely on your local device. All app functions, including the desktop pet animations and interactions, are processed locally on your computer without any data transmission to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics tools that might collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information from any users, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
